## One-paragraph truth

v0.2 is a **map and process** release: headline survey formalization coverage
remains **7 of 44** `EXACT`/`EQUIVALENT` rows; **one** additional row (BY-033
robot) has a **`RELATED`** formalization only (not headline coverage); **two**
survey rows carry **maintainer-reviewed** AI-facing bridges (BY-012 Rice/agent
packaging; BY-033 robot observer core under an explicit switching certificate).
Landscape ledger, literature-first packaging map, docs taxonomy, and bridge
lifecycle tooling are first-class. This release does **not** claim complete
survey coverage, EXACT robot-paper fidelity, formalization of BY-025
containment as its own Lean declaration, or that real deployed systems are
unverifiable.

## Critical non-claims

- **Reviewed bridges ≠ +2 headline coverage.** Robot is RELATED-only.
- **BY-012 `REVIEWED`** applies to the **AI-facing bridges**
  (`Verification.rice`, `AgentBehavior.no_behavioral_safety_verifier`), not a
  re-audit of Mathlib’s classical Rice proofs as novel AI-safety content.
- **BY-033** remains **RELATED** to van Leeuwen & Wiedermann: Lean assumes
  `SwitchingConstruction`; SPA/Def. 3 are not fully mechanized. Scoped
  interpretation is accepted; slogans like “robot ethics is impossible” are not.
- **Alfonseca / BY-025:** the Rice **pattern** is available via AgentBehavior;
  the survey row itself is still **MAPPED** (no dedicated Lean artifact).
- **Melo et al.:** related literature + packaging via BY-012; not a Table-1
  source and not a third formalization of the same object.
- Reviews are **maintainer** statement + scoped interpretation acceptance.
  External domain review is welcome for later releases.
- Public provenance after merge is the **squash commit on `main`**, not private
  pre-squash v0.1 approval ancestry.
